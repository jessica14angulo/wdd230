Target Audience:

Our Target Audience for this website is anyone between 18-100 years who wants to know more about Crystal Clear Lake Chamber of Commerce and join the community. Our audience also includes anyone who wants to know our plans and receive more information about local atractions.

Personas

Cassandra Knight

Cassandra is a 26 years old college student who lives in the community and wants to open her own small bussiness when she graduates. She wants to know more about Crystal Clear Lake Chamber and how to become a member when she starts her bussiness. She thinks that, by joining our Chamber or Commerce, she will be able to reach to more people and find clients in our community.

Tom Wagner

Tom is a 23 year old neighbor in Crystal Clear Lake Chamber community. He wants to know more about activities and plans CCLC has, he wants to help with organization and he also wants to make friends in his community. He enters the website to see what plans our Chamber of Commerce has for his community.

Scenarios

  • Why is Crystal Clear Lake Chamber important for the community?
  • What benefits do members have?
  • Do you have Area atractions info for new visitors?
  • What type of bussinesses can join?
  • How can I schedule an informative meeting?
